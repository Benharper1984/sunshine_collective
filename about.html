About Kelly

Guiding you toward your brightest, most authentic self

My Mission

My mission is to empower individuals to discover their inner light, heal from past wounds, and create meaningful, purposeful lives aligned with their authentic selves.

I believe that every person has an innate capacity for healing, growth, and joy. Through compassionate guidance, evidence-based coaching techniques, and holistic wellness practices, I create a safe space for deep personal transformation. My work is rooted in the understanding that when we heal ourselves, we contribute to healing the world.

Meet Kelly Harper

Kelly Harper

Founder & Certified Wellness Coach

Kelly brings over 8 years of experience in wellness coaching and personal development. After experiencing her own transformational healing journey through mindfulness and holistic wellness practices, she felt called to share these life-changing tools with others. Her approach combines evidence-based coaching techniques with intuitive wisdom to create a safe, supportive space for deep personal growth.

Kelly holds certifications from the International Coach Federation (ICF), Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR), Reiki Master Practitioner, and Yoga Alliance RYT-200. When she's not coaching, Kelly can be found hiking in nature, practicing yoga, or curled up with a good book and her rescue dog, Sunshine.

My Approach

Holistic wellness grounded in compassion and evidence-based practices

Coaching Philosophy

My coaching is grounded in the belief that you are the expert of your own life. I serve as your guide and witness, helping you uncover your inner wisdom and authentic truth. My approach integrates mindfulness, somatic awareness, and positive psychology to support whole-person healing and growth.

I believe in meeting you exactly where you are on your journey, without judgment or agenda. Together, we create a sacred container for exploration, healing, and transformation that honors your unique path and timing.
